Frequently Asked Questions

How often is the weather updated?

Every 10 minutes.

How fast is support?

Typically within 24 hours.

Where does the weather data come from?

Our data is sourced from the OpenWeather API, a reliable and globally recognized weather provider.

Can I search for international cities?

Yes, you can search for weather updates in cities around the world using their name and country code.

What do the weather icons represent?

The icons represent different weather conditions such as clear skies, rain, snow, and thunderstorms. Hover over the icons for a description.

How do I change between dark, light, and high contrast modes?

You can toggle the theme using the icon in the header. The selected theme will be remembered on your next visit.

What do I do if the location isn’t found?

Double-check the spelling and try adding a country code. If the issue persists, contact support.

Does the app store any personal information?

No personal data is stored. We only save your last searched location using browser localStorage for convenience.

Can I use the app offline?

Some basic features may be visible, but live weather data requires an internet connection.

Is the app accessible for screen readers?

Yes, we use ARIA live regions and keyboard-friendly navigation to ensure accessibility for assistive technologies.
